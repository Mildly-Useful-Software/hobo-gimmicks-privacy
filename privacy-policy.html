Privacy Policy for Hobo Gimmicks

Last updated: 16.08.2025

Information We Collect

Hobo Gimmicks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All extension settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Theme preference (Light/Auto/Dark)
  • Debug logging setting
  • Force-enable gimmick preference setting
  • Extension enabled/disabled state

This data is synced across your Chrome devices if you have Chrome sync enabled. No data is sent to external servers.

Website Access

The extension only activates on Hobo domains to provide visual enhancements. It does not access or modify content on other websites.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not communicate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact mildly-useful-software@tancred.de.
